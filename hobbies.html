Avid Outdoorsman

Adam loves to be in nature. A frequent hiker, rock climber, and paddle-boarder, he can often be found wandering around the state parks of Central North Carolina, or paddling down the Deep or Rocky Rivers. Additionally, Adam is a licensed skydiver, having completed more than 30 freefall skydives and more than 60 total jumps.

Self-proclaimed Nerd

When he's not outdoors, Adam is a through-and-through nerd. Passionate about data, coding, and learning in general, Adam is frequently honing his skills on python, R, SQL, HTML, CSS, or Javascript. Additionally, he is an amateur drone builder and pilot, having built from scratch a 5" quadcopter.

Husband, Friend, Volunteer

Adam is married to his wife, Molly, who works as a graphic designer and oil painter (and is also a licensed skydiver). Adam serves as a board member for the Operation Allies Refuge Foundation, and has volunteered time to act and coach with Furman University's improv comedy troupe, and Augusta, Georgia's charitable improv comedy troupe Schrodinger's Cat.
